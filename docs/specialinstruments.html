Instrument Calibration at Microtech calibration Solutions Private Limited

Calibration laboratory provides calibration of instruments for ph meter, thermocouple, thermometer, ovens calibration, time measurement, conductivity meter, calibration of glassware, rtd calibration and other thermal instruments calibration.

From Standard Platinum Resistance Thermometers (SPRTs) with fixed-point cells aligned to the International Temperature Scale (ITS-90), achieving precision from -80°C to +1200°C and up to -196°C for cryogenic ranges, to thermocouples calibrated up to 1200°C — our primary temperature standards and reference systems are capable of calibrating virtually any type of thermocouple or thermometer.

With over 15 years of experience in temperature calibration, we help industries maintain precise and accurate temperature process controls. Our comprehensive calibration services also cover a wide range of specialized instruments including time, revolution, analytical, thermal, and humidity measurement devices.

Our customers rely on our laboratory for calibration of flow meters, thermocouples, thermometers, RTDs, temperature calibrators, temperature baths, time and sound meters, RPM meters, conductivity meters, TDS meters, furnaces, and glassware, as well as other thermal calibration instruments. Each calibration is performed using advanced reference standards under controlled environmental conditions to ensure the highest level of accuracy and reliability.

To learn more about how you can achieve the same level of measurement precision through our accredited calibration laboratory — including adjustments, data recording, and recall notifications — contact us today. We provide calibration services for all the instruments listed above and many more.
