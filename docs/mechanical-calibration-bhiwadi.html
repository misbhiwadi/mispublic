Instrument Calibration at Microtech calibration Solutions Private Limited

Calibration laboratory provides calibration of instruments for pressure gauges, flow meter, pipette, mass, weights, calibrated weights, glass wares, burettes, torque wrenches, gauge, vernier caliper, scales, gauges, test sieves, vacuum gauges

Dimensional and Pressure Calibration Services

From micrometers, thread gauges, plug gauges, and length rods to a wide range of other precision measuring tools and standards, we go the extra mile to ensure exceptional accuracy and reliability in every calibration.

Our Dimensional Calibration Laboratory is maintained at a precise temperature of 20°C to ensure measurement stability. Each tool undergoes thorough cleaning, inspection, and calibration as per standard procedures. We provide detailed calibration data and recall notifications as part of our service to help you manage calibration schedules efficiently.

Backed by extensive experience in ISO consultancy and quality documentation, our calibration reports are designed to provide process consistency, traceability, and repeatability — ensuring your equipment maintains long-term performance accuracy.

By combining strict environmental control, advanced laboratory equipment, and a team of experienced metrology professionals, we deliver pressure calibration services that you can trust again and again.

Our laboratory is equipped with pneumatic pressure standards ranging from vacuum to 1,000 psi and hydraulic standards up to 40,000 psi, with accuracies as high as 0.0025% (25 ppm). We offer both standard and custom calibration services for a wide range of pressure measuring devices including:

  • Pressure Gauges
  • Pressure Transducers and Transmitters
  • Pressure Calibrators and Controllers
  • Monitors and Deadweight Testers

Our customers rely on our calibration laboratory for the calibration of various instruments including pressure gauges, flow meters, pipettes, mass and weights, glassware, burettes, torque wrenches, vernier calipers, scales, test sieves, and vacuum gauges, all performed using our traceable calibrated masters.
