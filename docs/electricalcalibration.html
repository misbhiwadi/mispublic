Electro-Technical Calibration Services | NABL Traceable & Onsite Support

We offer comprehensive electronic and electro-technical calibration services for a wide range of instruments — including precision reference standards, multimeters, oscilloscopes, frequency counters, power supplies, and portable calibrators. Our calibration solutions are designed to restore your test and measurement equipment to accurate, reliable performance with minimal downtime.

Whether at our fully equipped calibration lab or directly at your onsite location, each instrument is calibrated following standardized procedures to ensure repeatable, NABL-traceable results. Detailed calibration data and reports are maintained in observation sheets and securely stored for a minimum of four years for full traceability.

Our highly skilled calibration engineers are available 24×7, 365 days a year, delivering precise and professional service tailored to your industry needs. We specialize in calibration of meggers, flow meters, transmitters, conductivity meters, pH meters, thermocouples, multifunction calibrators, resistance meters, ammeters, and other electro-technical instruments.

We utilize advanced Fluke calibrators and other high-precision reference instruments, ensuring exceptional accuracy and confidence in every calibration. Our Best Measurement Capabilities (BMC) meet stringent industry standards, making us a trusted choice for companies seeking reliable and NABL-traceable calibration services in India.

For more information contact us today.

How to take our Calibration Services for your instruments and test devices

  • How to Take Our Calibration Services for Your Instruments and Test Devices

    To begin, send a detailed list of your instruments and test devices — including their range and accuracy specifications — to our Microtech Calibration Center. Our team will review your requirements and provide a quotation for calibration, either in our laboratory or at your site, based on your needs.

    Please specify whether you require a calibration certificate with NABL logo or without NABL logo. When issuing your purchase order, clearly include the following information where applicable:

    • (a) Requirements for approval of product, procedures, processes, and equipment
    • (b) Requirements for qualification of personnel
    • (c) Quality management system requirements

    If an accredited calibration service is required, please mention “NABL Accredited Calibration” on your purchase order to ensure proper documentation.

    To schedule an on-site calibration, arrange a visit from our service calibration technician through our coordinator by calling our office at (+91 9636055399). You may select the appropriate combination of technical expertise and service days that best fit your requirements.

    Coordinate with your instrument users to arrange a suitable workspace on the scheduled days. Our Microtech technicians will make necessary security arrangements and stage the instruments for calibration.

    Calibration technicians require a clean, well-lit workspace with stable electrical power and controlled temperature conditions to meet the required environmental standards as per our documented procedures.

    Please ensure instruments are properly shutdown or disconnected before calibration and provide full access to our team. We use high-precision Fluke multi-product calibrators for performing accurate and traceable calibrations. After completion, our technicians will affix calibration stickers to each instrument.
