About Us

Overview

MicroTech Industrial Solutions, incorporated in 2019 in Bhiwadi, is an emerging leader in integrated industrial and architectural services. We specialize in a wide range of solutions including Aluminium and MS fabrication, Fire Doors, Instrument Calibration, and on-demand hardware supply. With a commitment to quality and innovation, our aim is to bring multiple essential services under one reliable umbrella.

Aluminium Fabrication & Architectural Solutions We offer comprehensive aluminium fabrication services such as Aluminium Office Partitions, Doors & Windows, Sound-proof Windows, Aluminium Cabinets, False Ceilings, Machine Covers, Structural Glazing, and Modern Kitchen Interiors. In addition, we undertake complete glass solutions including plain glass, toughened glass, mirror glass, and modern elevation façades to enhance both residential and commercial spaces with contemporary aesthetics.

Mild Steel (MS) and Stainless Steel Fabrication MicroTech Industrial Solutions is also engaged in MS and Stainless Steel fabrication services covering industrial structures, decorative metal works, wiring support frameworks, machine covers, and tin-shed construction with boundary wall fencing. Our expert workmanship ensures strength, safety, and long-term durability for all structural metal applications

NABL-Approved Calibration & Testing Services We are actively involved in offering calibration and testing services through NABL-approved laboratories, ensuring accuracy and compliance. We cater to Mechanical, Thermal, and Electro-Technical calibration requirements. Our testing capabilities include Air Quality, Stack Emissions, Work Zone Environment, Water and Wastewater, Pressure Vessels, Lifting Machines, Hoists, and more — meeting national standards for industrial safety and performance.

Hardware & Electrical Equipment Supply We provide procurement and supply solutions for a wide variety of industrial hardware, architectural fittings, and electrical equipment based on client demand. Our strong vendor network enables timely delivery, competitive pricing, and compatibility with modern fabrication systems, serving as a dependable sourcing partner for projects of all scales.
