Instrument Calibration at Microtech calibration Solutions Private Limited

Calibration laboratory provides calibration of instruments for pressure gauges, pipette, mass, weights, flow meter, glass wares, calibrated weights,vernier caliper,burettes, torque wrenches, gauge, scales,vacuum gauges, gauges, test sieves

NABL Accredited Instrument Calibration Services

We provide calibration services for over 2,000 types of instruments across diverse measurement areas including pressure, vacuum, thermal, mass, volume, dimensional, force, time, and electrical parameters. Our facility is equipped to handle instruments of all ranges and least counts, ensuring high precision and reliable results.

We take pride in offering NABL-accredited calibration services for the majority of instruments we handle. Each calibration is performed in accordance with ISO/IEC 17025:2017 standards, and the NABL logo is included on certificates where applicable.

Clients can choose from multiple calibration options:

  • Onsite Calibration at client facilities
  • Laboratory Calibration by submitting instruments at our lab
  • Industry Calibration Camps conducted at client locations

For more details, you can download our NABL accreditation certificate from the quick link below or from the official NABL website.

To receive a customized instrument calibration quotation, please send us your request via email at sales@ncrindustrialsolution.in.
